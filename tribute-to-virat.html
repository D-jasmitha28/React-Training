Tribute to Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and one of the best batsmen in the world. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he has become a symbol of excellence in cricket. Kohli has broken numerous records and is known for his aggressive batting style and remarkable consistency. For more information, visit Wikipedia.
